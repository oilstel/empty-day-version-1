Empty Day is a day of social media silence taking place each year on October 11, 2019. There are 191 days until the next Empty Day. It’s a day we all agree to leave social media alone. Instead we all agree to connect, reconnect, and maybe even go outside ... we “go quietly” for a day.

Empty Day is also a website where one can find tools, assignments, and notes for using and replacing social media with other activities or just use these networks in more relaxing ways. So Empty Day doesn’t disregard social media altogether ... instead it focuses on how and when we post in order to promote better well-being and mental health for all.

To get involved, you can:

  • Tell your friends. Feel free to write to them or call them.
  • Organise an event (asking people to leave their phones at home).
  • Stay off social media on 11th October.

Empty Day is not a company, charity or any other organisation. It isn’t owned by anybody or anything. It aims to be peaceful and genuine.
